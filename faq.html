Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, pens, vials, and research guidance.

How do I place an order?
Simply add your chosen products to the cart, proceed to checkout, and enter your delivery details. You will receive a confirmation email with **bank transfer payment instructions**.
What payment methods are accepted?
We currently accept **UK bank transfer only** for privacy and security reasons. Full payment details are sent directly to your email once checkout is completed.
When will my order be dispatched?
✅ Orders placed and paid **before 3PM** are dispatched **same day**. ✅ Orders after 3PM dispatch the following working day.
Do you offer next-day delivery?
Yes — all UK orders are sent via **tracked next-day delivery** once dispatched.
Is packaging discreet?
Absolutely. All orders ship in **plain unbranded packaging** with no reference to contents.
What is included with Pens?
✅ Every **Pen order includes 10 brand-new sterile pen heads** as standard. ✅ Pens are supplied ready for use in research protocols.
What is included with Vials?
✅ Every **Vial order includes 10 sterile syringes**. ✅ All vials are supplied **pre-reconstituted and ready for research use**.
Do you provide dosage guidance?
We provide **research-based example dosage and unit calculators only**. These are strictly for **laboratory research reference purposes** and **not medical advice**.
What happens if I abandon checkout?
If checkout is abandoned, you may receive a **secure recovery email** allowing you to resume your order later.
Do I need an account?
No — guest checkout is allowed. However, creating an account lets you view **previous orders, delivery history, and saved details**.
Do you accept returns or refunds?
Due to the nature of research products, **we cannot accept opened returns**. If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us immediately.
Are these products for human consumption?
❗ **No. All products are STRICTLY supplied for laboratory research purposes only.** ❗ They are **NOT approved for human consumption**. ❗ By ordering, you confirm that you understand and accept this.
